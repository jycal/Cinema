INFORMATION AND TERMS

  • The age rating of 16 years is strictly adhered to! Under 16? Then no access to performances for 16 years and older, not even accompanied by an adult. For verification, identification may be requested.
  • Online paid transactions can be canceled free of charge until the start of this performance via your Starlight Cinema account or via the Starlight Cinema customer service. If tickets are canceled at the box office of the cinema, paid tickets will be compensated by means of Starlight Cinema free tickets. Theseats will then become available again.
  • When purchasing tickets, the QR code of the ticket must be scanned. These are easy to find in your Starlight Cinema account.
  • No rights can be derived from the chosen seat numbering. Due to circumstances it is possible that the chosen numbering is canceled and another seat is assigned.
